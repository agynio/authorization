1. Get the release namespace:

  {{- if .Release.Namespace }}
  $ kubectl get pods -n {{ .Release.Namespace }}
  {{- else }}
  $ kubectl get pods
  {{- end }}

2. To connect to the gRPC service locally:

   $ kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "service-base.fullname" . }} 50051:{{ (index .Values.service.ports 0).port }}

   Then connect your client to localhost:50051.
